Q:   What the program does?
Ans: This program works to find the common element from 
     three different sorted arrays.

Q:   What is it's sample input and output?
Ans: 
                       OUTPUT
     Enter the size of first array
     7
     Enter array elements
     2  6  5  1  3  8  9
     Enter the size of second array
     6
     Enter array elements
     4  7  10  5  11  15
     Enter the size of third array
     5
     Enter array elements
     5  2  7  16  11
     
     5 is common in all three arrays.

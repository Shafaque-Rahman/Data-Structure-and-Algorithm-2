
Q:   What the program does?
Ans: For a given array with positive and negative numbers,it will find 
     the contiguous sub-array with maximum sum.

Q:   What is the sample input and output?
Ans:                          
                                  OUTPUT
     Enter the size of array
     7
     Enter array elements
     -5  -2  4  1  -3  6  -1
     Maximum sum of contiguous array is 8.
